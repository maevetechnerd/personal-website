Possible Questions

  • Why C, for the back end?

C is a language that I was drawn by in the early to mid part of 2021 when I built my first computer. My best friend who was on a journey on learning what Linux was, dragged me into it as well and I started researching myself. During the same time my friend decided to teach me some programming. I did have a fixed mindset back then, but it changed the more my friend helped pushing me over to the growth mindset. I started also watching videos of Linus Torvalds where he did some speaking on events and interviews. Both Linus and my best friend pushed me to actually for real starting to program. So here I am now. Learning C because I love the concept of controlling the computer with its low level capabilities and the fact you can build almost anything with it. I also plan on learning Assembly because I really want to become a low level pro. I know there are much easier programming languages to use for the back end, but this is my personal website. This is a website I experiment with and do personal changes to increase my skills and also make my website fancier. I do know though, that my website could become slow and bloated because of my experimentation.
